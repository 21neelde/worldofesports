Over the time of Super Smash Bros.' history, there have been four overpowering characters for each game that completely

overshadow the rest of the cast: Pikachu in Smash 64, Fox in Smash Bros. Melee, Meta Knight in Smash Bros. Brawl, and

Bayonetta in Smash 4. Quite a large margin of the competitive scene mainly uses these characters to give themselves

better results, which makes sense since the goal of the game is indeed to win. The reason these characters are so good

is mainly because they have one major thing in common: speed. While obvious in Melee's Fox and not so obvious in Smash 4's

Bayonetta, they all use this to their advantage to overwhelm their opponent.
