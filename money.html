eSports players make a lot more money than most people would think. According to Business Insider, each League of Legends Championship player must be paid a minimum of $12,500 for one 28-game season. This is clearly a pretty decent amount of money, but some players make a bit more than that. Team Liquid, an eSports team with over one thousand tournaments with their players in attendance has earned over $18 million.
