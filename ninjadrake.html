Ninja and Drake playing Fortnite and breaking Twitch records

You may be surprised to hear that Ninja and the popular music artist Drake have collaborated to bring the viewership of

Twitch a special treat in playing Fortnite. At one point, they broke a twitch record, having over 230,000 viewers

at one point, a crazy amount for anyone to have. Another interesting thing about this livestream is that Drake

mentioned that if the Fortnite developers were to add in a dance from a song of his to the game, he would reference the

game in one of his songs.
